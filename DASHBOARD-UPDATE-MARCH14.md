## Decision Tiers Explained

### 🟢 STRONG BUY (Risk Score > 70 + positive confluence)
- **What to do:** Enter with full position (5% of portfolio)
- **Context:** Wave is bottoming, multiple metrics agree, stress is low
- **Hold:** 3-5 days typically
- **Example:** Wave at +0.2, momentum rising, Energy > 0.5, Stress < 0.3

### 🟢 LIGHT BUY (Risk Score 60-70 + 3+ metrics agree)
- **What to do:** Enter with half position (2.5% of portfolio)
- **Context:** Wave is rising but not confirmed yet
- **Hold:** 3-5 days, watch for confirmation
- **Example:** Wave at +0.0, rising, but Attractor < 1.3

### 🟠 TAKE PROFITS (Risk Score 60-70 + SELL signal)
- **What to do:** Sell 50-75% of position
- **Context:** Wave approaching peak, momentum weakening
- **Keep:** 25-50% for continuation
- **Example:** Wave at +0.7, Stress rising, Momentum turning negative

### 🔴 STRONG SELL (Risk Score > 75 + SELL signal)
- **What to do:** Exit all longs, consider short if experienced
- **Context:** Wave at extreme, multiple exhaustion signals
- **Timeline:** 1-2 day move typically before reversal
- **Example:** Wave at +0.85-0.95, Curvature > 1.8, Risk Score > 75

### 🟡 HOLD/WAIT (Risk Score 35-50)
- **What to do:** Stay in current position or cash
- **Context:** Undefined direction, cycles misaligned
- **Timeline:** Wait for clarity, don't force entries
- **Example:** Wave at -0.1 to +0.1, Attractor < 1.2

### 🟢 ACCUMULATE (Risk Score < 35)
- **What to do:** Build position slowly (scale in 1-2% at a time)
- **Context:** Bearish washout, capitulation zone
- **Expected:** Bounce forming within 5-10 days
- **Example:** Wave at -0.5, Risk Score 25, Stress < 0.4

## Reading the Backtest

### Why Backtest Matters

The backtest answers: **"Does this system actually work, or is it just lucky?"**

If ASTRO return > Buy & Hold return = **system adds value**  
If ASTRO return < Buy & Hold return = **system underperforming** (be cautious)

### Components

**ASTRO System Return:**
- Colored 🟢 green if outperforming hodl
- Colored 🟡 yellow if close to hodl
- Colored 🔴 red if underperforming hodl
- Shows % return if you traded every signal

**Buy & Hold Return:**
- Baseline (no trading)
- Colored 🔵 blue regardless
- Shows what you'd get if you just held

**Win Rate:**
- Percentage of trades that made money
- >60% = excellent
- 45-60% = good (losses are small)
- <45% = losing system (skip it)
- Green if trading works, orange if mediocre

**Avg Trade Duration:**
- Days per trade on average
- Astro typically 3-7 days (good for swing trading)
- >14 days = slow system
- <1 day = scalping (stressful)
- Green if in the 3-7 day sweet spot

## Confidence Level Explained

The "Confidence Level" (0-100%) shows how many key metrics agree on the current signal.

### How It's Calculated

**4/4 agreement (100%):**
- ✅ Signal matches direction (BUY/SELL)
- ✅ Risk Score is extreme (>75 or <35)
- ✅ Wave momentum confirms
- ✅ Stress is aligned (low for BUY, high for SELL)

**3/4 agreement (75%):**
- ✅ Signal is there
- ✅ Risk Score supports it
- ✅ Wave is clear
- ❌ Stress doesn't fully align

**2/4 agreement (50%):**
- ✅ Signal exists
- ✅ Wave is moving the right way
- ❌ Risk Score is neutral
- ❌ Stress is misaligned

**1/4 agreement (25%):**
- ✅ One metric supports it
- ❌ Everything else says "wait"

### Trading With Confidence

- **>75% confidence:** Full position size
- **50-75% confidence:** Half position size
- **25-50% confidence:** Micro position (0.5-1%)
- **<25% confidence:** Skip the trade
