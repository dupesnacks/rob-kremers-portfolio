The Problem: Recovery in the Dark

Every year, 800,000 Americans suffer a stroke. They spend a week in the hospital, get discharged with a few physical therapy sessions, and are left to manage their recovery alone. No monitoring. No early warnings. No visibility into what's actually happening.

25%

Readmission Rate within 30 days of discharge (mostly preventable)

15%

Second Stroke Risk within 1 year (silent failure)

6.5M

Stroke Survivors in the US with no recovery monitoring

$20B+

Annual Market in post-acute stroke care and rehabilitation

The Core Issue: Recovery outcomes are determined in the dark. Patients decline silently until they're in the ER. Families don't know what to watch for. Doctors only see snapshots every 4-6 weeks.

The Solution: Connected, Intelligent Monitoring

StrokeGuard connects the devices stroke survivors already have (Apple Watch, glucose monitors, PT apps) and uses AI to detect early warning signs before they become crises.

🚨 Early Warning Alerts

Detect declining recovery patterns before the crisis: AFib episodes, medication non-compliance, depression signals, cognitive decline, infection markers.

📊 Unified Dashboard

All recovery data in one place: heart rate, glucose, activity, sleep, blood pressure, PT progress. Real-time visibility for patient, family, and clinician.

🤖 AI Coaching

Personalized daily guidance based on recovery trajectory: "Your movement patterns show you're progressing slower than typical. Here's a customized PT routine."

📈 Predictive Risk Scoring

ML models predict readmission risk and second stroke probability within 30-90 days. Suggest preventive interventions before escalation.

👨‍⚕️ Clinician Integration

Neurologists and therapists get auto-alerts when patients at risk. Actionable insights, not just noise. Easy integration with existing EHR systems.

🔄 Family Engagement

Family members get progress updates and specific, actionable suggestions to support recovery. Transparent, data-driven coaching.
