Why Aurora. Why Now.

Aurora's mission to deliver the benefits of self-driving technology safely, quickly, and broadly resonates deeply with me—not only as a brand marketer, but as someone who has spent years translating complex autonomous vehicle technology into credible, compelling narratives for diverse audiences.

I bring over 15 years of experience leading brand strategy and integrated campaigns across global brands and frontier technology companies. Most recently at TuSimple (now CreateAI), an autonomous semi-truck company, I led brand and narrative development during a pivotal stage of growth. A core part of my role was translating highly technical Level 4 autonomous driving systems into strategic communications for regulators, fleet operators, investors, industry analysts, and potential partners. I developed campaign frameworks, product storytelling, executive messaging, and cross-functional toolkits that aligned marketing, safety, and government relations around a unified brand voice.

What This Means for Aurora: I've already walked the path Aurora is on. I understand the unique challenge of building trust in autonomous technology—where brand credibility is survival. I know how to navigate the complexity of speaking to regulators, industry partners, fleet operators, and the public simultaneously. I know how safety messaging, regulatory positioning, and brand aspiration intersect.

Earlier in my career, I built global brand systems at Nike and adidas, including leading the development of Nike's Global Basketball Retail Brand Playbook and contributing to global footwear campaigns for athletes like Damian Lillard and James Harden. In those roles, I executed multi-channel, 360° marketing programs from strategic brief through launch and measurement—partnering closely with creative teams, agencies, and cross-functional stakeholders to ensure brand consistency and commercial impact.

The opportunity at Aurora uniquely combines both sides of my background: deep brand stewardship within a complex, high-stakes industry and integrated campaign leadership at scale.

I am particularly excited about:

  • Developing integrated brand strategies around major company moments and product milestones—turning technical achievements into moments that matter to customers, partners, and the industry
  • Partnering cross-functionally with Safety and Government Relations to shape trusted, forward-looking narratives that align regulatory excellence with brand aspiration
  • Building a compelling employer brand that attracts top technical talent in an increasingly competitive AV landscape—showing the world what it means to build the future
  • Leveraging AI tools to scale insight development, campaign optimization, and creative ideation—working smarter, not just harder

Throughout my career, I have operated as a strategic advisor to executives and cross-functional teams—bringing clarity to ambiguity, structure to complex initiatives, and discipline to campaign execution. I thrive in environments where technology, policy, and brand intersect.

Aurora is not simply building a product—it is shaping a new transportation ecosystem. I would welcome the opportunity to steward and elevate a brand that carries such consequential impact.

Thank you for your consideration. I look forward to the opportunity to discuss how my experience in autonomous technology and global brand leadership can contribute to Aurora's next chapter.
