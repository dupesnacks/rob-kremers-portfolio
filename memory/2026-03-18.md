## Technical Findings

### Zebra ZP450 Printer Driver Solution
**Status:** SOLVED - Don't build custom drivers  
**Finding:** The Zebra ZP450 (2009 model) is a legacy printer that doesn't expose as a standard USB HID device to Windows. Initial attempt to build custom Python/C# driver failed because printer wasn't detected by pyusb/pywinusb libraries.

**Solution:** Use **Seagull Scientific drivers** (free download from bartendersoftware.com)
- Current version: 12.0 (Jan 2026)
- Size: 114MB
- This is the industry standard for Amazon FBA sellers
- Printer detected and working with proper Windows integration

**Lesson:** Don't reinvent the wheel for legacy hardware. Seagull Scientific maintains production-grade drivers for thermal printers that the vendor (Zebra) itself doesn't support in modern contexts.

## Legal Research - Tesla Lawsuits

### 1. Justine Saint Amour v. Tesla, Inc. (FSD Crash)
- **Court:** Harris County District Court, Texas
- **Filed:** Late February 2026
- **Status:** Ongoing
- **Incident:** August 18, 2025 - Cybertruck with FSD engaged attempted to drive off overpass on I-69 Houston
- **Key Detail:** Driver DID disengage FSD, but vehicle had too much momentum to stop
- **Damages Sought:** $1M+ + punitive
- **Attorney:** Bob Hilliard (Hilliard Law)
- **Unusual Allegation:** Tesla was negligent in "hiring and retaining Elon Musk as CEO" due to his design decisions (rejected LiDAR)

### 2. Nyree Hinton v. Tesla, Inc. (Odometer Fraud)
- **Court:** U.S. District Court, Central District of California
- **Filed:** April 2025
- **Dismissed:** March 12, 2026 (Judge granted Tesla's motion)
- **Plaintiff Claim:** Odometer readings inflated 15-117% via hidden algorithms
- **Real Issue:** Plaintiff was confusing **estimated range display** with actual odometer readings
- **Tesla's Defense:** Three independent sensor sets showed only 0.2% variance (within 4% industry standard)
- **Outcome:** Case completely dismissed; plaintiff declined Tesla's offers for joint testing/inspection

**Pattern:** Both cases involve misunderstanding of how Tesla systems work vs. actual fraud. FUD circulation is real, but discovery of facts tends to resolve them.
