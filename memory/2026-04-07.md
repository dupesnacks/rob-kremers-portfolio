### Architectural Decisions

   - Solves problem of arbitrage noise dominating trend signals
   - Allows hybrid system: fast + slow timeframes visible, properly weighted
   - Reusable for any outfit subtype (arbitrage, political, mathematical, etc.)

   - No hardcoded timeframe restrictions in engine logic
