## Architectural Decisions & Patterns

### 1. Period Weighting Formula (CRITICAL PATTERN)
**Problem:** REPEATER_9 (MA9, MA18, MA27, MA54) was firing thousands of times per scan, drowning out institutional signals on MA200+ and MA420+.

**Solution:** Implement `log2(period) / 6.5` scaling in engine.py:
- MA9 ≈ 0.5x weight
- MA54 ≈ 0.9x weight  
- MA100 ≈ 1.0x weight (baseline)
- MA420 ≈ 1.3x weight
- MA818 ≈ 1.5x weight

**Result:** Institutional large-period signals now surface naturally without being buried in short-period noise.

**Implementation:** `period_scale = math.log2(max(period, 2)) / 6.5` in `detect_hits_for_ticker()` → multiply weight by period_scale before recording hit.

**Why this works:** Acknowledges that rare, structural signals (MA400+) are inherently more significant than frequent micro-oscillations (MA9-MA54). The logarithmic scale is gentle enough to not eliminate short periods entirely, but strong enough to create proper signal hierarchy.

---

### 2. Deduplication Strategy (NEW PATTERN)
**Problem:** Same price event (e.g., NVDA touching MA256 at $120.50) appears on 5m, 15m, 30m, 1h charts simultaneously, inflating hit counts artificially.

**Solution:** One hit per (ticker, outfit, sma_period, calendar_date) — keep the highest-weight instance.

**Implementation:** `_dedup_hits_by_date()` function in engine.py. Called after all timeframes scanned for a ticker but before returning results.

**Effect:** Reduces signal noise dramatically. Historical example: TSLL scan went from synthetic 15K+ signals to meaningful institutional patterns only.

### 3. Alert Detection System (NEW SUBSYSTEM)
**5 Alert Types** (detect_alerts in alerts.py):

1. **CONFLUENCE** — 3+ different outfits firing on same ticker
   - Weight: outfits × high-period hit count
   - Severity: critical if 5+ outfits or 5+ high-period hits

2. **HIGH_PERIOD_CLUSTER** — 3+ MA200+ hits on one ticker
   - Captures institutional activity (large-period SMAs)
   - Severity: critical if max_period ≥ 700 or 6+ hits

3. **OUTFIT_SURGE** — One outfit active on 5+ tickers
   - Detects when single pattern sweeps market sector
   - Severity: critical if 8+ tickers

4. **AFFINITY_LOCK** — Ticker's primary-affinity outfit fires on MA400+
   - Highest conviction signal (outfit + ticker alignment + large period)
   - Severity: critical if period ≥ 700

5. **MAGNETIZED_HIGH** — Multi-session consecutive hit on MA200+
   - Tracks sustained programs (e.g., active trades)
   - Severity: critical if period ≥ 500 or 4+ sessions

**Architecture:** Alerts returned as `[{level, type, ticker, outfit, summary, details}]` via `/api/alerts` endpoint. Frontend displays critical/high alerts prominently at top of dashboard.
