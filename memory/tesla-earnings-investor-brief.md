## ✅ Positive Surprises (Near-Term Catalysts)

### Margin Expansion
- Gross margin jumped to **21.1%** — highest in a while
- Up 478bps year-over-year
- Even beat Q4 2025 (20.1%)
- **Driver:** FSD subscription push, tariff refunds, manufacturing efficiency

### Market Demand Rebound
- APAC demand continuing to grow
- South America growth accelerating
- EMEA and North America showing rebound
- **Implication:** Geographic diversification reducing China/US exposure risk

### Cash Generation
- **$3.9B operating cash flow** in one quarter
- **$1.4B free cash flow** (beat expectations of negative)
- Company is generating money despite capex explosion
- **Implication:** Self-funding growth; no dilution risk

## 📝 Caveats (For Balanced View)

**Noted by analysts on X:**
- Q1 deliveries: 358,023 (missed by ~7,600 units)
- Inventory buildup: 50,000 more cars built than sold
- Energy deployment down 38% sequentially (though still growth YoY)
- Some margin gains from "one-time warranty/tariff benefits" (sustainability question)

**Investor Takeaway:** Beat is real, but some timing benefits + inventory buildup suggest Q2 may be flatter.
