**Operational Value:** Watch BTC support/resistance levels as leading indicators for TSLA rebalancing signals.

### 3. Spot vs Leverage Rotation Strategy
**Extended History Insight:**
- Institutions accumulate in leverage FIRST: TSLL (1,862w), TSLR (1,337w), TSLT (804w) = 3,916w leveraged long
- Maintain static hedge: TSLQ (1,736w) = risk management layer
- Spot accumulation SECOND, triggered by macro confirmation: TSLA (241w) spot only became bullish on BTC breakout

**Pattern:** Leverage = convection building, spot = macro confirmation trade. Entry ladder.

### 4. Hedge Ratio as Information Signal
**Discovery:** Hedge ratio changes ≠ directional reversal indicators; they signal capital reallocation efficiency.
- TSLQ -112.5w allocation = "we have enough downside insurance, moving capital to spot"
- NOT: "we're exiting the hedge" or "reducing conviction"
- Interpretation rule: Watch hedge ratio drift, not absolute level
