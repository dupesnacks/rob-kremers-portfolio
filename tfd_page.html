🦷Thomas Family Dentistry

Patient Engagement & Practice Management MVP

Beaverton, Oregon

💡The Opportunity

A small practice management platform purpose-built for 4-person dental teams to:

  • Keep patients engaged between visits with automated reminders & follow-ups
  • Handle coverage gaps when the hygienist is out sick (on-call marketplace)
  • Reduce no-shows through intelligent scheduling & patient alerts
  • Simplify operations for the wife/ops manager
  • Drive consumables revenue (whitening, retainers, care products) directly through the practice

⚠️The Current Problems

Problem 1: Hygienist Coverage Gaps

The Issue: When your wife or the hygienist is sick, there's no easy way to find a substitute. This means:

  • Patients have to be rescheduled (creates friction, some don't return)
  • Practice loses $200-400/day in hygiene revenue
  • Wife spends hours on the phone trying to find coverage
  • Patients get frustrated with the back-and-forth

The Data: ~15% of healthcare workers call out sick per year. For a 1-person hygiene team, that's ~50+ days of potential coverage gaps.

Problem 2: Scheduling & Rescheduling Chaos

The Issue: When the wife is late or unavailable to answer calls:

  • Patients can't reach the office (lost appointments/revenue)
  • Manual rescheduling is slow and error-prone
  • No visibility into who called or what they wanted
  • Staff has to remember who needs to reschedule

The Impact: Average dental practice loses 10-15% of potential revenue to no-shows and rescheduling friction.

Problem 3: Patient Engagement (The MARLO Angle)

The Issue: Patients don't remember when to come back. They either:

  • Forget to schedule their 6-month cleaning (practice loses recurring revenue)
  • Buy whitening/care products from Amazon instead of the practice
  • Get their retainers from mail-order companies
  • Switch to a "friendly" DSO chain because they feel more engaged

The Opportunity: Automated engagement keeps patients in YOUR orbit. They buy from you. They show up. They refer friends.

✅The MVP Solution

An Integrated Platform Built for Small Teams

A simple, mobile-first app that handles the 3 biggest pain points:

📞Smart Scheduling & Coverage

Auto-Find Substitute Hygienists

When someone calls out:

  • System automatically alerts pre-vetted substitute hygienists in the local network
  • First to accept gets the shift
  • Patients are automatically rescheduled or notified (not the wife's job)
  • Practice keeps $300+ that would be lost revenue

📅Patient Self-Service

Reduce the Load on Operations

Patients can:

  • View available appointment slots online
  • Reschedule themselves (if hygienist is out)
  • Book next appointment during checkout (habit stacking)
  • Get SMS/email reminders (reduces no-shows)

💬Automated Patient Engagement

Keep Patients Coming Back (MARLO-Style)

Automatically:

  • Send 6-month cleaning reminders
  • Post-procedure follow-ups ("How's your cleaning feeling?")
  • Educational content (whitening benefits, oral care tips)
  • Promotion alerts (special offers on whitening, retainers)

🛍️In-Practice Consumables Shop

Capture Revenue Being Lost to Amazon

Patients can buy:

  • Professional whitening kits (vs. OTC)
  • Replacement retainers (vs. mail-order)
  • Toothbrush subscriptions
  • Care products (mouth rinse, floss, etc.)
