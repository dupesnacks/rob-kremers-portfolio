Why This Change?

Raul's Apr 9 2026 Clarification:

"No individual stock operates on a singular outfit. [...] The only equities that have a relevant system are [SPX, IXIC, DJI, VIX]. [...] on a leg higher I recorded GME operating on the 420 outfit on the daily chart. The next time it's a manufactured higher it could be on any other outfit. Old Approach Problem: Restricting TSLA to 420 meant missing TSLA legs on Octuple (816), Octane (818), etc. Restriction = blind spot.

New Approach Solution: Scan all outfits, weight historical observations (420 for TSLA) 2× via is_affinity(). Catch new legs while maintaining emphasis on known relationships.

Implementation:

CANONICAL_SYSTEM_TICKERS = {"SPX", "IXIC", "DJI", "VIX"}

def outfits_for_ticker(ticker: str) -> set[str] | None:

        return TICKER_OUTFIT_MAP[ticker]
