ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
-------------------

The 1m timeframe produces HIGH-FREQUENCY hits because:
  1. MA16 on 1m resets every ~16 minutes
  2. On indices (SPX/IXIC/DJI), price touches MA16 constantly
  3. Each touch = 1 hit, weight = pos_weight * affinity_mult * period_scale
  4. period_scale for MA16 = log2(16)/6.5 ≈ 0.62 (relatively low)
  5. But 14 tickers × 4 timeframes (1m/3m/5m/15m) × 6 periods = many opportunities

Example: DIA 1m scan finds MA16/MA31/MA63 hits on EVERY candle → rapid accumulation

Deduplication (_dedup_hits_by_date) reduces noise but doesn't eliminate it:
  - Dedup by (ticker, outfit, sma_period, date)
  - Still allows multiple hits per outfit per ticker per day
  - Result: DIA gets 96 active signals (high count) vs previous ~42


DECISION OPTIONS
----------------

A) KEEP AS-IS
   - Russia Pres arbitrage signals integrate into main ranking
   - Pros: Full transparency, no hidden signals
   - Cons: Arbitrage dominates trend analysis, SSO loses credibility
   - Risk: Daily rankings become unstable (jump based on 1m whipsaw)

B) DOWNWEIGHT ARBITRAGE HITS (50% multiplier)
   - Add logic: if outfit.subtype == "arbitrage", multiply weight by 0.5
   - Pros: Keeps arbitrage visible but secondary, smooths rankings
   - Cons: Somewhat arbitrary multiplier, requires code change
   - Risk: Might under-represent actual HFT significance

C) SEPARATE ARBITRAGE SIGNALS
   - Exclude arbitrage from total_count (used for ranking)
   - Keep arbitrage hits in "key_levels" and "top_hits" (visible but not ranked)
   - Pros: Cleanest separation, preserves trend ranking integrity
   - Cons: Requires output schema change, more complex ranking logic
   - Risk: Users might miss important HFT signals

D) RESTRICT RUSSIA PRES TO 1M ONLY
   - Remove 3m/5m/15m from timeframes constraint
   - Only scan 1m for this outfit
   - Pros: Reduces noise ~70%, keeps true 1m HFT focus
   - Cons: Might miss multi-timeframe confluence signals
   - Risk: Loses 15m confirmation that Raul used (SSO short @ 15M@53.34)

E) EXCLUDE FROM LIGHT SCAN
   - Remove Russia Pres from TICKERS_LIGHT affinity
   - Keep in TICKERS (full scan) only
   - Pros: Light scan stays clean (trend only), full scan gets arbitrage
   - Cons: Two different datasets (light vs full) might confuse users
   - Risk: Light scan loses HFT context entirely
