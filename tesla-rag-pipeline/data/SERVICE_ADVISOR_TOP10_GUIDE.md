# Tesla Service Advisor - Top 10 Issues & Correction Codes by Model

## 🎯 Quick Reference Guide for Common Service Requests

## 🚗 MODEL Y - TOP 10 ISSUES

### 1. **Squeaky Suspension (Control Arms/Lateral Links)** ⭐ MOST COMMON
**Issue:** Creaking/squeaking from front suspension, especially when turning or over bumps
**Root Cause:** Worn upper control arms, lateral links, compliance links
**Correction Codes:**
- `3101030001` - Front Upper Control Arm - RH (Remove & Install) - 0.45 hrs
- `3101030002` - Front Upper Control Arm - RH (Remove & Replace) - 0.45 hrs
- `3101030401` - Front Upper Control Arm - LH (Remove & Install) - 0.54 hrs
- `3101030402` - Front Upper Control Arm - LH (Remove & Replace) - 0.54 hrs
- `3101030301` - Front Lower Lateral Link (Remove & Install) - 0.42 hrs
**Typical Labor:** 2-3 hours for full suspension refresh

### 2. **Rearview Camera Failure**
**Issue:** Camera shows black screen or error message
**Root Cause:** Camera module failure or connection issue
**Correction Codes:**
- `17220500101` - Camera - Rear Facing (Remove & Install) - 0.12 hrs
- `1722040501` - Cover - Camera - Rear Facing (Remove & Install) - 0.06 hrs
- `1722040502` - Cover - Camera - Rear Facing (Remove & Replace) - 0.06 hrs

### 3. **Brake Issues / Brake Pad Replacement**
**Issue:** Low brake pads, grinding noises, or soft pedal
**Root Cause:** Normal wear or thermal damage
**Correction Codes:** Search database for "brake rotor" + "front" or "rear" specific codes

### 4. **Window Regulator Malfunction**
**Issue:** Window stuck, moves slowly, or won't open/close
**Root Cause:** Motor or regulator mechanism failure
**Correction Codes:**
- `11500010` - Window Regulator (Recalibrate) - 0.06 hrs
- `1150010031` - Window Regulator Motor - Front - LH (Remove & Install) - 0.18 hrs
- `1150010032` - Window Regulator Motor - Front - LH (Remove & Replace) - 0.24 hrs

### 5. **Forward Collision Avoidance (Software)**
**Issue:** False alerts, system disabled, or camera errors
**Root Cause:** Software glitch or camera misalignment
**Correction Code:** Requires software diagnostics (check Tesla diagnostic system)

### 6. **Touchscreen/MCU Malfunction**
**Issue:** Unresponsive touchscreen, black screen, or reboot loops
**Root Cause:** Software corruption or MCU module failure
**Correction Code:** Requires MCU diagnostic and potential replacement (labor varies 1-4 hours)

### 7. **Battery/Thermal Management**
**Issue:** Battery warning, reduced power, or overheating
**Root Cause:** Thermal sensor malfunction or cooling system leak
**Correction Codes:**
- `18304353` - MY Thermal Management - Cooling System Test/Adjust - 0.36 hrs

### 8. **Tire/Wheel Alignment/Replacement**
**Issue:** Uneven wear, alignment needed, or tire damage
**Root Cause:** Normal wear or impact damage
**Correction Codes:**
- `34014005` - Seasonal Wheel and Tire Set Swap - 0.50 hrs
- `34014505` - Accessory Wheel/Tire Package Assembly - 1.08 hrs

### 9. **Door Latch/Alignment Issues**
**Issue:** Door doesn't latch properly or has large gaps
**Root Cause:** Latch mechanism failure or hinge misalignment
**Correction Codes:**
- `1133020101` - Door Latch - Front - LH (Remove & Install) - 0.66 hrs
- `1133020102` - Door Latch - Front - LH (Remove & Replace) - 0.66 hrs

### 10. **Headlight Module Failure**
**Issue:** Headlight out, dim, or intermittent
**Root Cause:** Module failure or connector issue
**Correction Codes:**
- `17400800719` - Headlights (Check Adjustment) - 0.12 hrs
- `17130100119` - Headlights (Check Alignment) - 0.18 hrs

## 🚗 MODEL S - TOP 10 ISSUES

### 1. **Air Suspension Compressor Failure** ⭐ MOST COMMON
**Issue:** Vehicle sits low, compressor constantly runs, or suspension warning
**Root Cause:** Compressor seal failure or relief valve stuck
**Correction Codes:**
- `31102001` - Compressor - Air Suspension - 1st Gen (Remove & Install) - 1.38 hrs
- `31102002` - Compressor - Air Suspension - 1st Gen (Remove & Replace) - 0.72 hrs
- `31102301` - Compressor - Air Suspension - 2nd Gen (Remove & Install) - 0.60 hrs
**Typical Labor:** 1-2 hours

### 2. **MCU/Infotainment Malfunction**
**Issue:** Touchscreen black, frozen, or slow
**Root Cause:** MCU module failure (very common on older Model S)
**Correction Code:** MCU replacement required (contact Tesla)

### 3. **Battery/Thermal Management Issues**

**Root Cause:** Thermal sensor malfunction
**Correction Code:** Requires full diagnostic

### 4. **Door/Falcon Door Alignment**

**Root Cause:** Motor or hinge mechanism wear
**Correction Code:** Requires mechanical inspection

### 5. **Brake System Degradation**

**Root Cause:** Brake fluid contamination or pad wear
**Correction Codes:**
- `33000004` - Diagnosis: Brake System - 0.50 hrs

### 6. **Window Motor/Regulator Failure**
**Issue:** Window stuck, moves slowly, or won't work
**Root Cause:** Motor burnout or regulator wear
**Correction Codes:**
- `11502001` - Window Motor/Regulator Assembly - Front - LH (Remove & Install) - 0.60 hrs
- `11502101` - Window Motor/Regulator Assembly - Front - RH (Remove & Install) - 0.60 hrs

### 7. **Steering Column Issues**

### 8. **Charging System Problems**
**Issue:** Charger malfunction or slow charging
**Root Cause:** Connector corrosion or module failure
**Correction Codes:**
- `18303701` - Hose - Rocker to Charger (Remove & Install) - 0.80 hrs
- `18303702` - Hose - Rocker to Charger (Remove & Replace) - 0.96 hrs

### 9. **Light Module Failures**
**Issue:** Headlights or taillights out
**Root Cause:** Module electrical failure
**Correction Code:** Requires lighting module diagnostics

### 10. **Suspension Height Sensor Error**
**Issue:** Suspension warning or height adjustment failure
**Root Cause:** Sensor misalignment or electrical fault
**Correction Code:** Requires suspension diagnostics

## 🚙 CYBERTRUCK - TOP 10 ISSUES

### 1. **Panel Gaps/Stainless Steel Alignment** ⭐ MOST COMMON
**Issue:** Visible gaps between body panels, misaligned doors, or uneven trim
**Root Cause:** Stainless steel rigidity makes adjustment difficult
**Correction Code:** Search for "panel alignment" or "door adjustment" codes
**Note:** This is a known issue - manual alignment required, labor-intensive

### 2. **Tonneau Cover Malfunction**
**Issue:** Cover won't open/close, stuck, or misaligned
**Root Cause:** Motor or rail misalignment
**Correction Code:** Tonneau cover motor/mechanism codes (search database)

### 3. **Trim/Molding Misalignment**
**Issue:** Trim pieces not flush, sharp edges, or rattling
**Root Cause:** Manufacturing tolerances on stainless steel
**Correction Code:** Trim adjustment/replacement codes

### 4. **Stainless Steel Dent/Finish Repair**
**Issue:** Visible dings, dents, or scratches on body
**Root Cause:** Impact damage (stainless shows everything)
**Correction Code:** Body shop codes for dent/polishing work

### 5. **Charging System Problems**
**Issue:** Charger won't connect or slow charging
**Root Cause:** Port connector issue
**Correction Codes:**
- `1519023121` - Wireless Charger - Center Console (Remove & Install) - 0.12 hrs
- `1519023122` - Wireless Charger - Center Console (Remove & Replace) - 0.12 hrs

### 6. **Brake Issues**
**Issue:** Brake noise, reduced stopping power, or warning lights
**Root Cause:** Brake pad wear or rotor damage
**Correction Codes:**
- `33000004` - Diagnosis: Brake System - 0.50 hrs
- `33010100` - Brake Rotor - Front - LH (Measure Runout) - 0.35 hrs

### 7. **Window Regulator Failure**
**Issue:** Window stuck, grinds, or won't move
**Root Cause:** Motor failure or regulator wear
**Correction Codes:**
- `1150040011` - Window Regulator - Front Door - LH (Remove & Install) - 0.36 hrs

### 8. **Suspension/Air System**
**Issue:** Suspension too low, compressor noise, or system fault

- `3110008011` - Compressor - Air Suspension (Remove & Install) - 0.84 hrs

### 9. **Touchscreen/Electronics**
**Issue:** Unresponsive screen, black display, or software issues
**Root Cause:** MCU failure or software glitch
**Correction Code:** MCU diagnostics/replacement (contact Tesla)

### 10. **Door/Handle Issues**
**Issue:** Door alignment, handle broken, or latch failure
**Root Cause:** Cybertruck geometry or handle mechanism wear
