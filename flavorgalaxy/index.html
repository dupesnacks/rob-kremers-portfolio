What is Flavor Galaxy?

A game, not a battle.

Flavor Galaxy turns the struggle of selective eating into an exciting journey through space. Your child picks an explorer character and travels through 8 cosmic planets, each unlocking new sensory challenges.

Instead of forcing bites, they progress through 5 sensory phases (Look ‚Üí Touch ‚Üí Smell ‚Üí Taste ‚Üí Swallow), earning points, levels, and celebrating every milestone.

The SOS Approach

Clinically-validated. Neuroscience-backed.

The Sequential Oral Sensory (SOS) Approach is used worldwide by speech-language pathologists and occupational therapists. It's not about forcing kids to eat‚Äîit's about rewiring their sensory system.

  • LOOK (0 days) ‚Äî Getting used to seeing the food
  • TOUCH (3+ days) ‚Äî Feeling textures with hands
  • SMELL (5+ days) ‚Äî Exploring aromas (primes taste buds)
  • LICK (7+ days) ‚Äî Trying small tastes without swallowing
  • TASTE (10+ days) ‚Äî Eating and enjoying

Each phase takes time. We don't rush. Your child's nervous system needs time to integrate each new sensation.
