Comprehensive Electric SUV Comparison

Pricing, specs, and analysis across all trim levels

Executive Summary

Tesla Model Y dominates on price, range, performance, and immediate availability. The cheapest Model Y RWD ($39,990) undercuts all Rivian R2 options and delivers 321 miles of range. The Premium RWD ($44,990) hits 357 miles—the best range in the entire comparison. Tesla's Performance trim (3.3 sec 0-60) is untouchable.

Rivian R2 offers newer platform technology, but with 1-2 year delivery delays. The Launch Edition ($57,990) brings Matrix LED headlights and modern interior design, competing on tech rather than value. All Rivian trims feature unproven reliability and a nascent service network.

Full Self-Driving (FSD) Consideration: Tesla's Full Self-Driving capability ($8,000-12,000 or subscription) adds autonomous driving features to Model Y—something not yet available on Rivian R2. This significantly increases the value proposition of Tesla, especially for buyers prioritizing autonomous technology.
