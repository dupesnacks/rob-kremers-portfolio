📊 Final Verdict

Tesla Model Y dominates on price, range, performance, and availability. Every price tier offers better value than comparable Rivian models, with the Tesla Model Y Premium RWD ($44,990) hitting 357 miles of range—beating the best Rivian by 27 miles at a lower price.

Rivian R2 offers newer platform technology and Matrix LED headlights as differentiators, but comes to market 1-2 years later with unproven reliability and a developing service/charging network.
