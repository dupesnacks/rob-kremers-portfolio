The Bottom Line

Tesla Model Y dominates on price, range, performance, and immediate availability. The cheapest Model Y RWD ($39,990) undercuts all Rivian R2 options and delivers 321 miles of range. The Premium RWD ($44,990) hits 357 miles—the best range in the entire comparison. Tesla's Performance trim (3.3 sec 0-60) is the fastest.

Rivian R2 offers newer platform technology, but with 1-2 year delivery delays. The Launch Edition ($57,990) brings Matrix LED headlights and modern interior design. However, all Rivian trims feature unproven reliability and a nascent service network.

Full Self-Driving (FSD): Tesla now offers FSD at $99/month subscription (no longer $8-12k upfront). Rivian R2 has not announced autonomous driving features yet. This is a major differentiator favoring Tesla across all trims.
