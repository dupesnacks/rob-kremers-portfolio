4.6 Fundamental Misunderstandings to Correct

❌ MISCONCEPTION #1: Cycles Are Universal

What you might think: These celestial cycles work because they're universal laws.

Reality: Markets are driven by human psychology, not the heavens. These cycles work IF traders are aware of them and trade accordingly. They're a self-fulfilling prophecy.

Implication: The system's effectiveness depends on adoption. If 1M traders all use Astro, the cycles strengthen. If adoption drops, signal quality degrads.

❌ MISCONCEPTION #2: 90-Day Forecast Is Risk-Free

What you might think: Since cycles are deterministic, I can predict 90 days ahead perfectly.

Reality: Cycles predict WHEN momentum will shift, not HOW MUCH price will move. Black swans, news events, and regime changes break cycles.

Implication: Forecast tells you "BUY on March 28", but can't predict March 28's price. Use forecast for TIMING, not PRICE TARGETS.

❌ MISCONCEPTION #3: Fixed Thresholds Are Optimal

What you might think: BUY when wave > -0.2 always works.

Reality: Optimal thresholds vary by market regime, volatility, and asset. DOGE might need wave > -0.1, while BTC needs wave > 0.0.

Implication: Use machine learning to find adaptive thresholds, not fixed constants.
