## The Big Picture

The forecast predicts **future BUY and SELL windows** by analyzing the deterministic celestial cycles.

The three cycles repeat on fixed astronomical periods:
- **Metonic:** 231 days (19-year lunar-solar alignment)

These are **not random**. They're mathematical sine waves. So future wave values are **perfectly predictable** 90+ days in advance (barring real-world volatility).

## Why "Predict" Works for Cycles

### The Trap: Price Prediction is Hard

Cycles can't predict *what price will be*. They can predict:
- **Direction changes** (when momentum will flip)
- **Volatility clusters** (when chop will be highest)
- **Exhaustion points** (when reversals are likely)
- **Window openings** (when setups will align)

### The Edge: You Know When to Enter/Exit

Rather than "DOGE will be $1.00 on March 28," you know:

> "On March 28, the Apsidal cycle will cross zero, combining with Solar to create a +0.3 wave. That's a BUY signal. Enter at $0.12 with 3-5 day hold. Expect 5-15% upside depending on real volatility."
