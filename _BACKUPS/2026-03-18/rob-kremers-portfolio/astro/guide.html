Risk Score (0-100)

The Risk Score is your primary decision metric. It represents how risk-on or risk-off market conditions are.

Score Range State Meaning What To Do
0-20 🔴 STRONG RISK-OFF Chaotic, unstable, high volatility, no clear direction AVOID ENTRIES — Wait for stabilization
20-35 🟠 MODERATE RISK-OFF Cautious market, cycles misaligned, weak momentum HOLD — Not a good entry time
35-50 🟡 NEUTRAL Balanced, could go either way, undefined direction WAIT — Requires additional confirmation
50-70 🟢 MODERATE RISK-ON Positive momentum, cycles aligning, good entry conditions BUY SMALL — Moderate confidence
70-100 🟢🟢 HIGH RISK-ON Hot market, strong directional bias, cycles highly aligned AGGRESSIVE ENTRY — Best conditions

Risk Score Formula

Risk Score = 50 + (wave × 30) + (energy × 20) - (volatility × 30) + (confidence × 0.2)

What Each Component Does:

  • Wave × 30: Positive wave = bulls winning. Negative = bears.
  • Energy × 20: High momentum in the trend direction.
  • Volatility × -30: High volatility = risky. Reduces the score.
  • Confidence × 0.2: When the signal is very certain, risk score rises.
Example Calculation:
Wave: +0.8 (bullish) = +24
Energy: 0.7 (strong) = +14
Volatility: 0.3 (calm) = -9
Confidence: 85% = +17
Total: 50 + 24 + 14 - 9 + 17 = 96 (VERY HOT!)

Physics Metrics (8D Analysis)

Six core metrics that measure different aspects of market structure:

Energy (0.0 - 2.0+)

Price momentum × volatility. How much "force" is driving the market.

Range Interpretation
0.0-0.2 Dead market, no directional force
0.2-0.5 Moderate movement building
0.5-0.8 Strong momentum, excellent for entries
0.8+ Very high momentum (or potential exhaustion)

Attractor (0.5 - 2.0)

How well the three celestial cycles are aligned. High = cycles "pulling" together.

Range Meaning
0.5-1.0 Cycles misaligned, chaotic
1.0-1.5 Starting to align
1.5-2.0+ Cycles highly synchronized — STRONGEST CONDITIONS

Phase Accel (0.0 - 1.0)

Acceleration of the Solar Cycle. Shows if market is shifting into a new phase.

Range Meaning
0.0-0.1 No acceleration, steady state
0.1-0.3 Mild acceleration, transitioning
0.3-0.5 Strong acceleration, phase shift
0.5+ Extreme acceleration, major regime change coming

Curvature (0.5 - 2.5)

The "bend" of the price path. How much the market is turning.

Range Meaning
0.5-1.0 Linear movement, straight trend
1.0-1.5 Moderate turn, normal behavior
1.5-2.0 Strong turning point, potential reversal
2.0+ Very high curvature = Top or Bottom

Memory (τ) (0.6 - 1.0)

Historical momentum inertia. 0.69 is the base constant (Astro's numerology).

Range Meaning
0.69 Base resonance constant
0.69-0.80 Weak memory, market forgetting past trends (whipsaw risk)
0.80-1.0 Strong memory, trend continuing

Stress (0.0 - 1.0)

Market friction and instability. High stress = turbulent conditions.

Range Meaning Action
0.0-0.2 Calm, stable BEST for entries
0.2-0.5 Moderate tension Good conditions
0.5-0.8 High stress, choppy Be careful with position size
0.8+ Extreme chaos AVOID or take profits

Celestial Cycles

Three astronomical-period cycles that synchronize to create trading opportunity windows:

Metonic Cycle (231 days)

Period: 19 years (lunar-solar alignment) | Range: -1.0 to +1.0

  • +0.5 to +1.0: Bullish phase
  • -0.5 to -1.0: Bearish phase
  • Watch for: Crossing zero = start of new alignment phase

Apsidal Cycle (109 days)

Period: 9 years (orbital precession) | Range: -1.0 to +1.0

  • +0.7 to +1.0: Strong uptrend
  • -0.7 to -1.0: Strong downtrend
  • Note: Fastest cycle = short-term directional bias

Solar Cycle (134 days)

Period: 11 years (sun activity) | Range: -1.0 to +1.0

  • Medium-term cycle (between Metonic and Apsidal)
  • Extreme values (+/- 0.9+): Phase Accel spike = reversal warning

Combined Wave (The Key Metric)

All three cycles smoothed to a 12-day moving average:

Wave Value State Signal
< -0.5 Deep bearish SELL / SHORT
-0.5 to -0.2 Bearish HOLD
-0.2 to +0.2 Neutral WAIT
+0.2 to +0.5 Bullish building Look for BUY
+0.5 to +0.7 Strong bull BUY
> +0.7 Extreme bull (potential top) SELL / Take profits
The Critical Rule:
✓ Wave crosses from negative to positive = PRIME BUY ZONE
✓ Wave crosses from positive to negative = PRIME SELL ZONE
✓ Watch Wave Momentum (derivative) to confirm the crossover

Trading Signals

🟢 BUY Signal

Minimum Conditions (all 3 must be true):

1. Wave > -0.2 (not in deep bearish)
2. Momentum > 0 (wave moving upward)
3. Energy > 0.3 (sufficient force)

Best Entry Conditions:

  • Wave > +0.2 (clearly bullish)
  • Momentum > +0.1 (strong acceleration)
  • Energy > 0.7 (tons of momentum)
  • Attractor > 1.4 (cycles synchronized)
  • Stress < 0.3 (calm market)
  • Risk Score > 65

Position Sizing:

  • Risk Score 50-65: 25% position size
  • Risk Score 65-80: 50% position size
  • Risk Score 80+: 100% position size

🔴 SELL Signal

Conditions (either one):

1. Wave > +0.7 (potential exhaustion/top)
2. Wave > +0.4 AND Momentum < -0.05 (rolling over)

Best Exit Conditions:

  • Wave at +0.8 to +0.9 (extreme overbought)
  • Momentum has turned sharply negative
  • Curvature > 1.8 (strong turning point)
  • Stress rising (increasing instability)
  • Risk Score < 40 (flipping to risk-off)

🟡 HOLD Signal

Default Conditions:

  • Wave in neutral zone (-0.2 to +0.2), OR
  • Energy too low to trade on, OR
  • Risk Score 35-50 (undefined direction)

What To Do:

  • Stay in cash
  • Set alerts for BUY/SELL conditions
  • Don't FOMO enter
  • Wait for clear signal

🚫 Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Trading on HOLD signals — Risk Score 35-50 is dangerous
  • Ignoring Stress — High stress (>0.7) makes all signals unreliable
  • Not watching Wave Momentum — Wave value alone isn't enough
  • Over-trading at extremes — Wave +0.9 or -0.9 is reversal risk
  • Ignoring cycle desynchronization — When Attractor drops, get out

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Use Stress as a filter — Skip trades when Stress > 0.6
  • Short holds — 3-5 days typical, not a hold-forever system
