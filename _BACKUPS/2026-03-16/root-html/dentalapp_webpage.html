3. Patient Leakage Problems (3-Part Revenue Drain)

Problem 1: Appointment Leakage

Issue: Patients forget cleanings, don't schedule follow-ups, or switch to DSO chains

  • Typical dentist practice: 15-25% no-show rate
  • Patient forgets appointment → Lost revenue + seat utilization waste
  • Patient ghosting → Likely switched to competitor

Solution: Automated appointment reminders (SMS/email/app), intelligent scheduling based on clinical timeline, post-procedure follow-up automation

Impact: 10-15% reduction in no-shows = 5-8% revenue lift

Problem 2: Consumables Leakage

Issue: Patients buy retainers, whitening kits, toothbrushes, floss from Amazon/mail-order instead of dentist

  • Retainer orders: SmileDirectClub/Byte steal $200-400/patient/year
  • Whitening kits: OTC/online market = $7.7-13.5B, mostly DTC instead of practice
  • Toothbrush/floss subscriptions: Quip, Burst, etc. steal recurring revenue
  • Insurance handles diagnostic, but patient buys maintenance elsewhere
🎯 The Specific Threat:
  • Clear aligner market: $4.2B (2023) → $32-46B by 2031 (24-30% CAGR)
  • Direct-to-consumer (DTC) segment growing fastest
  • SmileDirectClub/Byte: Lower cost, convenience, no dentist visit required
  • Independent dentist loses patient entirely or converts to low-margin visit

Solution: E-commerce platform sells branded consumables through practice (retainers, whitening, bite guards, denture care kits, toothbrush subscriptions)

Impact: 20-30% revenue lift from consumables sales ($240K on $1.2M practice base)

Problem 3: Relationship Leakage (Between-Visit Ghosting)

Issue: Without touch points, patients lose habit of returning to dentist

  • Patient finishes procedure → Months pass → Patient eventually tries DSO for cleanings
  • Invisalign/ortho patients drop off after treatment → No retention revenue
  • Insurance changes → Patient doesn't know how to navigate → Switches practice

Solution: Automated post-procedure check-ins, recall campaigns, pre-appointment engagement (educational content), insurance guidance

Impact: 15-20% improved patient retention + higher lifetime value

4. Insurance Complexity (Unique Dental Advantage)

Dental insurance is MORE complex than optometry:
  • Multi-plan coordination
  • Deductible tracking
  • Benefit year rollover confusion
  • Pre-auth requirements (orthodontia, major work)
  • Claim denials more frequent than eye care
  • Patient pain point: "Does my insurance cover this?" → Often results in patient switching

Solution:

  • Pre-appointment insurance verification
  • Real-time eligibility checking
  • Out-of-pocket cost estimates
  • Claims tracking & appeals automation
  • Payment plan coordination (CareCredit, etc.)

Impact: 10-15% improvement in conversion (fewer "let me think about it")
