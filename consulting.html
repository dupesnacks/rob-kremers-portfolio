What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an AI agent platform that automates your business operations. Think of it as hiring a tireless team member who works 24/7 without taking a day off.

Whether it's responding to customer inquiries, managing invoices, processing orders, scheduling appointments, or posting to social media, OpenClaw handles it. Intelligently. Consistently. Without mistakes.

The result? Your team gets back 10-15 hours per week. Your overhead goes down. Your business scales without hiring more people.

And the best part: it integrates with the tools you already use. Slack, email, spreadsheets, your CRM. OpenClaw learns your business and fits right in.

Common Questions

How long does implementation take?

Depends on complexity. Simple automations (like email responders) can be live in days. More complex workflows take 2-4 weeks. We'll map it out in the initial consultation.

What if I'm not technical?

You don't need to be. That's the whole point. You describe what you want, OpenClaw handles it. During setup, I'll make sure everything integrates with your existing tools.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your data lives in your systems (Slack, email, spreadsheets, etc.). OpenClaw doesn't store sensitive data. Everything runs transparently and you maintain full control.

What tools do you work with?

OpenClaw integrates with most modern tools: Slack, email, Zapier, Make, webhooks, APIs, spreadsheets, CRMs. If you use it, OpenClaw probably works with it.

How much does it cost?

Let's discuss in your free consultation. We'll scope the work and give you a clear price. Most small business automations are $2-10K depending on complexity.

What if I need changes later?

Easy. Your business evolves. We can update automations as you grow. Think of me as your operational partner, not just a one-time project.
