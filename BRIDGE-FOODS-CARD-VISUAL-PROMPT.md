# Bridge Foods Card - Visual Generation Prompt (Gemini/DALL-E 3)

## Master Prompt (Use This for Card 3 Visual)

```
Create a 1080x1920px mobile card illustration with transparent/dark starfield background.

LAYOUT (Vertical progression):
- TOP SECTION (Safe Food Zone):
  Show a "Safe Food" label with CRACKERS prominently displayed
  Surround with small icons of other foods kid might like (grapes, carrot, bread, etc.)
  Use warm golden lighting to highlight the safe food area

- CENTER SECTION (Bridge Food Hub):
  Place a PRETZEL (the bridge food) dead center as THE FOCAL POINT
  Make it LARGE and GLOWING with cyan neon outline
  Around the pretzel, show 3-4 sensory attribute labels radiating outward with glowing lines:
    • "Crunchy" (with sound wave lines)
    • "Salty" (with salt crystal iconography)
    • "Warm" (with heat glow effect)
  These attributes should connect the safe food ABOVE to the new food BELOW
  Show visual "paths" (cyan glowing lines/arrows) flowing from safe food through pretzel attributes to new food

- BOTTOM SECTION (New Food Zone):
  Show BREADSTICKS as the "New Food" label
  Surround with other foods to try (rolls, toast, etc.)
  Use cooler lighting, but still warm and inviting

VISUAL STYLE:
- Dark starfield background (#0a0a1e) with distant galaxies, shooting stars
- NEON GLOWING EFFECTS: Cyan (#00d9ff) and gold (#d4af37) glowing lines connecting foods
- Illustrated food items (cartoon style, rounded aesthetic, warm lighting)
- Glowing halos around foods, especially the bridge food (pretzel)
- Sensory attribute badges with cyan borders, glowing text
- Mouth/taste icon (glowing) at the CENTER where sensory traits meet, showing the bridge concept
- Arrows or flowing light paths showing progression: Safe → Sensory Matching → New
- Overall feel: Strategic, intelligent, hopeful

TEXT LABELS (keep minimal):
- "Safe Food: Crackers" (label at top)
- Sensory traits: "Crunchy", "Salty", "Warm" (radiating from center)
- "New Food: Breadsticks" (label at bottom)

BRAND COLORS:
- Background: Dark purple/navy starfield
- Primary glow: Cyan (#00d9ff)
- Secondary: Gold (#d4af37)
- Accent: Warm orange/peach for food lighting
- Text: White and cyan

MOOD:
- Educational yet magical
- Shows STRATEGY (not random)
- Emphasizes SENSORY CONNECTION (crunchy→crunchy, salty→salty, warm→warm)
- Inspires parent confidence
- Space/galaxy theme consistent with app brand

KEY VISUAL CONCEPT:
The pretzel in the CENTER is the KEY — it visually demonstrates that bridge foods share sensory qualities with safe foods, bridging the gap to new foods. The glowing lines and sensory labels make it OBVIOUS that this is a smart, strategic approach (not just guessing what food to try next).

SIZE: 1080×1920px (mobile portrait)
STYLE: Illustrated, rounded edges, neon glow effects, space-themed
FORMAT: PNG with transparent background (optional) or dark starfield fill
```

---

## Why This Works:

✅ **Visual Storytelling:** Safe Food → Bridge Food (Sensory Hub) → New Food (clear progression)
✅ **Shows Strategy:** The sensory labels radiating from the bridge food prove it's NOT random
✅ **Parent Confidence:** Parents immediately understand "oh, it matches what they like, so they're more likely to accept it"
✅ **Neon/Space Aesthetic:** Fits the Flavor Galaxy brand perfectly
✅ **Mobile-Optimized:** 1080×1920px is ideal for phone screens
✅ **Minimal Text:** Headlines on card, visual does the heavy lifting

```
SAFE FOOD: Crackers ────→ SENSORY MATCH ────→ NEW FOOD: Breadsticks

Crunchy • Salty • Warm (attributes labeled along the connecting path)
```

This is simpler but less visual. Stick with the HUB concept (what your image shows) for maximum clarity.
